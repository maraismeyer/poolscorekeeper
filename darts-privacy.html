Privacy Policy

Darts Score Pro

🔒 The Short Version

Darts Score Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data stays on your device. We have no access to it whatsoever.

Information We Collect

Darts Score Pro does not collect any personal information. The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Player names you enter
  • Match results you record
  • Game statistics derived from your matches

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not use any cloud storage, external databases, or third-party services to store your data.

If you delete the app, all data is permanently deleted from your device.

Third-Party Services

Darts Score Pro does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not integrate with any external APIs or services that could collect your data.

Children's Privacy

Darts Score Pro does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for family use.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our support page.
